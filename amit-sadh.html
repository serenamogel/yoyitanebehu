He also starred in “Running Shaadi” alongside Tapsee Pannu and in “Raag Desh” opposite Kunal Kapoor. Amit’s most recent movie is “Sarkar 3” along with Abhishek Bachchan, Amitabh Bachchan and Yami Gautam.
