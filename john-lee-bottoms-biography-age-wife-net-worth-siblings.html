John Lee Bottom, sometimes known as John Phoenix, was born in 1947 and became famous as the proud father of his talented children, River Phoenix, a deceased actor and singer, and Oscar-winning actor Joaquin Phoenix. He and his wife belonged to a religious group called The Children of God.

Robert Merril Bottom and Beulah Ernestine Ingram welcomed John on April 5, 1947 in Stanislaus County, California, in the United States. He was an American citizen and had the astrological sign of Aries.

Personal Life

John Lee was a husband and a father. His union with Arlyn Phoenix(his wife) produced lovely and famous kids. The pair first met in California, following which they began dating. On September 13, 1969, they got hitched.

After their wedding, they went on the road as missionaries, spreading the word of “The Children of God” in Mexico, South America, and other American states.

For a brief period, the newlyweds used the biblical names Jochebeb (Arlyn) and Amram (John).

For some time, their marriage was going wonderfully. In 1997, the couple decided to get a divorce. They were married for 27 years before filing for divorce.

The reason for the divorce is unclear. Subsequently, in 2001, his ex-wife married another man called Jeffrey Weisberg.

He was later diagnosed with cancer and fought it for a long time.

Scandal, Rumors & Controversies

John Bottom affiliated to “The Children of Group,” a controversial religious cult. John and his wife Arlyn joined the cult in 1973. The “Flirty Fishing” rules of the cult were prominent.


In the end, the couple lost faith in the Children of God and deserted it in 1977. Eventually, Arlyn would say she and her husband disagreed with the cult’s progressively twisted customs, especially the practice of flirtatious fishing.

What Killed John Bottom?

John Bottom gave up the ghost to cancer after fighting it for years after he was diagnosed.
