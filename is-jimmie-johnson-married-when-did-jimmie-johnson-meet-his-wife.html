When did Jimmie Johnson meet his wife?

Jimmie Johnson first met his wife, Chandra Janway in 2002. They were introduced to each other by Jeff Gordon. Gordon and Chandra knew each other because she was friends with Gordon’s wife, Ingrid Vandebosch.

Who is Jimmie Johnson’s family?

Jimmie Johnson is married to a woman named Chandra Janway. He was born to Gary Ernest Johnson and Catherine Ellen Johnson. He has two children named Genevieve Marie Johnson and Lydia Norriss Johnson. He has two siblings called Jessie Johnson and Jarit Johnson.

Jimmie Johnson age

How old is Jimmie Johnson? Jimmie Johnson is 46 years old.

Jimmie Johnson birthday

When does Jimmie Johnson celebrate his birthday? Jimmie Johnson celebrates his birthday every September 17.

When was Jimmie Johnson born? Jimmie Johnson was born on September 17, 1975.

How many wins does Jimmie Johnson have in his career?

Jimmie Johnson has a total of 84 NASCAR race wins in his career, 83 of which were in the Cup Series.
