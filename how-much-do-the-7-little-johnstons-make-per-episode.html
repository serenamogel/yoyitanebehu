Introduction to the 7 Little Johnstons

The 7 Little Johnstons are a family of seven that stars in the TLC reality show of the same name. The show follows the lives of Amber and Trent Johnston and their five children, all of whom have Achondroplasia Dwarfism. Since its premiere in 2015, the show has gained a significant following, with viewers tuning in to watch the family navigate life’s challenges and triumphs. One of the questions that often arises among fans is how much the Johnstons earn per episode of their hit series.

Understanding Reality TV Economics

Before diving into the specifics of the Johnston family’s earnings, it’s important to understand the general landscape of reality TV show payments. Reality TV stars’ salaries can vary widely based on the show’s success, the stars’ negotiation skills, and other factors such as syndication deals and merchandise sales.

The 7 Little Johnstons’ Show Success

The 7 Little Johnstons has been a successful series for TLC, which often translates into higher pay for its stars. The show’s unique perspective on the everyday life of a family living with dwarfism has captured the interest of a broad audience, contributing to its longevity and popularity.
