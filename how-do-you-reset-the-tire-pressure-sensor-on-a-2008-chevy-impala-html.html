What is causing my tyre pressure indicator to illuminate when my tyres are perfectly fine?

Even when the tyres are properly filled, the pressure in a car’s tyres lowers in the cold weather, on average. This is due to the fact that matter compresses when it is cold, but matter expands when it is hot. As a consequence, the low tyre pressure signal is activated, although the tyres are good for a short period of time before disappearing after the tyres have warmed up.
