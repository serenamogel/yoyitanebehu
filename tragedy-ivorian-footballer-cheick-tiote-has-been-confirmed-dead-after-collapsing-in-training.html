Tragedy: Ivorian Footballer Cheick Tiote Has Been Confirmed Dead After Collapsing In Training

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, July 9, 2024

Cheick Tiote, Ivorian international footballer and former Newcastle player has been pronounced dead, with cardiac arrest being the possible cause. The defender collapsed while training with his team members in Beijing few hours ago (June 5), sparking a sorrowful mood worldwide.

According to UK Mirror, his agent Emanuele Palladino confirmed the news on Monday afternoon, saying: “I confirm that my client Cheick Tiote passed away today after collapsing in training with his club

 Beijing Enterprises.”

The 30-year old started his pro-football career in Belgium side Anderlecht, and then moved on to FC Twente in the Netherlands.

Tributes have been poured from all over the world to the talented player.
