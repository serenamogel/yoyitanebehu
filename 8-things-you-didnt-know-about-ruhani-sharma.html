8 Things You Didnt Know About Ruhani Sharma

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, February 17, 2024

➡ Ruhani Sharma Biography

An Indian actress, Ruhani Sharma, predominantly works in Telugu cinema where she gave outstanding performances in many films and got acclaimed. She got praised for portraying the role in the rom-com film named ‘Chi La Sow’ which was released in the year 2018. After making her debut, she never looked back and continued working. Additionally, she did modeling for many modeling agencies and got recognition for her modeling skills. Moreover, her elegant looks make her stand out among all. She comes from a Punjabi family, basically from Solan, HP, India. After completing her studies, she made her way to showbiz and also appeared in many Punjabi music videos as well. Among many hits, some of them are Classroom, Kudi Tu Pataka, and many others. While among movies, she is mainly known for ‘Kadaisi Bench Karthi’ and many others that are notable. However, let us have look at these unknown things to know more about the stunning actress!

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Ruhani Sharma

1- Famous Role

In 2018, the actress gave a brilliant performance in the Telugu debut film named ‘Chi La Sow’ which was directed by Rahul Ravindran. In the film, she was praised by everyone for giving an outstanding performance.

2- Multi-talented Star

Ruhani Sharma is known for being a multi-talented star as she is blessed with not just good looks, yet heart-winning acting skills and modeling talent that make her unique among the crowd. While her expressions are too good and much appreciated.

3- Popular Music Videos

Among many best-loved Punjabi music videos, Ruhani Sharma loved and praised by everyone after appearing in ‘Classroom’ and ‘Kudi Tu Pataka’ both released in the year 2013.

4- Worked in Hindi Language Films

Here you will be surprised to hear that the pretty awesome actress has worked in Hindi language films including Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, and Malayalam for which she got immense love and much fame during her career life.

5- Adventurous

The ravishing beauty is known for being so adventurous as she loves to get exposure while traveling. So, she never misses the chance to travel during her holidays where she not just enjoys the beauty of nature, yet explores more in-depth.

6- Loves Swimming

Ruhani Sharma loves swimming as she believes that swimming relaxes her overall body and relieves stress after doing a lot of work on the sets. Despite a busy schedule, she manages her time for swimming.

7- Favorite Actors

The actress admires the most celebrated actors of Bollywood and South Indian cinema, such as; Shahrukh Khan, Deepika Padukone, Madhuri Dixit Nene, and Diljit Dosanjh as they are known for their outstanding acting chops.

8- Other Favorites

Her favorite color is Black and grey. When it comes to her favorite food, she prefers to eat Rajma Chawal- a traditional Indian dish. Her favorite travel destination is Florida. And as she is a sports lover, so she loves watching cricket.
