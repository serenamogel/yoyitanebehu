Remy Ma | Music, Endorsements, and Controversies

Music

Remy Ma joined the Terror Squad and was fortunate to get featured in their second and final album, ‘True Story, ‘released on July 27, 2004.

Their first single, ‘Lean Back,’ topped the Billboard Hot 100, through which she got her breakthrough.

After her incarceration, she released her mixtapes’ The BX Files’, ‘Shesus Khryst’ and ‘Blasremy.’

She released the mixtape ‘I’m Around’ in 2014. In addition, Remy on the Rocks, a collection of her many recordings, was released on April 28, 2015.

In 2016, Fat Joe revealed he and Ma would be releasing a new record titled ‘Plato O Plomo’ together. It was officially announced on March 2, 2016.

On April 26, 2018, she was seen performing the song “Company,” featuring another Bronx singer, “A Boogie Wit Da Hoodie.” On June 12, 2018, she released the new song ‘New Thang’ featuring French Montana.’

Endorsements

Remy Ma is a widely followed hip-hop artist. Her fanbase is significant, and she has an Instagram account with more than 8.7 million followers.

She has endorsed many brands and products with her popularity, including Fashion Nova, Gucci, I Wear Glam, Karen Sabag, and Moschino.

She has promoted many products through her Instagram, which further strengthens her net worth.

Controversies

In August 2004, a fight erupted between Remy Ma and Foxy Brown. Remy’s refusal to show respect and seniority to Foxy in September 2004 was notable enough to spark a bug between them.

As a result, they began exchanging diss tracks.

Remy punched Foxy inside the Island Def Universal offices in late January 2005, and a few days later, she dissed Remy.

Their feud lasted until 2017, when Remy said she doesn’t like Brown but still enjoys her music.

The feud between Remy and Nicki Minaj began in 2007 after Ma heard Minaj’s song ‘Dirty Money’ and thought it dissed her, which Nikki didn’t agree to.

However, Ma stated in a 2010 interview that she felt the song was directed at her. After that, they immediately began to release verses and songs, dissing each other.

On February 25, 2017, Ma released a song called ‘Shelter,’ which contained a lot of allegations against Minaj’s personal and professional life.

After a week, she shared her concerns about being a woman in the profession. Following that, Nicki Minaj retaliated with a diss song titled ‘No Frauds,’ in which she accused Remy of making fake accusations.

Remy Ma | Career

Remy Ma was first featured on Big Pun’s album ‘Yeeeah Baby.’ After that, Big Pun mentored her until he died in 2000. Following this, she was mentored further by rapper Fat Joe.

Remy released her debut album, ‘There’s Something About Remy: Based on a True Story,’ in 2006. After that, her career took off rapidly, with the record selling over 35,000 units in the first week of its release.

Remy was imprisoned in 2007 for her participation in a shooting in New York.

She was sentenced to eight years in jail. However, she was freed in August 2014 after six years.

When she was freed, her career resumed, and she has since collaborated with celebrities such as DJ Khaled, Rick Ross, and Yo Gotti.

Remi Ma | Charity

Remy feels empathetic towards incarcerated people.

In October 2018, Remy launched The Remy Ma Collection, a clothing line with some percentage of the sales going to the Remy Ma Foundation, which helps women who have been falsely convicted.

3 Interesting Facts About Remy Ma

  • Remy Ma handed herself into the New York Police Department in July 2007, connected to a gunshot incident in which she was engaged. She was recognized as the shooter and sentenced to eight years in jail, which she served for six years before being released in 2014.
  • Remy married her fiancé, Papoose, while imprisoned in May 2008, but police called off the wedding after Remy was caught attempting to smuggle a key into prison and banned Papoose from visiting her for six months.
  • Remy Ma has three stepchildren with Papoose, as well as one kid from a prior relationship. On December 14, 2018, she gave birth to Reminisce MacKenzie, the couple’s first child together.
