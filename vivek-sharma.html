Vivek Sharma is a multi-talented person who flaunts his extraordinary amazing skills. The intelligent man is known for his acting, directing, and writing skills. Yes… you heard it right. He is a great actor, film director and a scriptwriter who is recognized for his amazing work and performances. His directorial debut “Bhootnath” starring the veteran actor Amitabh Bachchan, got popular. Vivek Sharma was born on 27th August 1969, Jabalpur, MP, India where he born, grew up and studied. The 50 years old man has proved himself the best and successful film director and scriptwriter who enthralled viewers and movie lovers with his exceptional stories.

Career

Vivek Sharma took the start of his career with one of the sought-after film director, Mahesh Bhatt and he worked as an assistant director along with him. He then worked as a chief assistant director with a Bilingual movie named “Criminal” and then worked as an Associate Director for most-liked movies including “Duplicate” starring Shahrukh Khan, Juhi Chawla, and Sonali Bendre. Later, he worked on “Angaaray”, Phir Bhi Dil Hai Hindustani” starring Shahrukh Khan and Juhi Chawla.

He also designed Post Production for the most popular movie “Kuch Kuch Hota Hai” in which Shahrukh Khan worked alongside Rani Mukherjee, Salman Khan, and Kajol. Also, Vivek Sharma helped Sharukh Khan to establish his own company named “Dreamz Unlimited” and later, changed the name into “Red Chillis”. Moreover, he has worked in more than 25 movies for which he got immense love from viewers.

In 2008, Vivek Sharma directed the movie (debut directorial) “Bhootnath” starring Amitabh Bachchan. In 2009, he then directed another movie named “Kal Kis Ne Dekha Hai” and since then, he directed several movies for which he got huge acclaim.

Apart from acting and directing, he is blessed with writing skills and has written few scripts for movies including “Love You Hamesha” released in 1999, “Bhootnath” released in 1998, and “Kal Kis Ne Dekha Hai” released in 1999. However, the great man has accomplished whatever he desired during his career life.
