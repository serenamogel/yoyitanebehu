Skyler Joseph Clipner is better known as Skyler Stone is an American actor and starred in a television series Con (2005) which ran on Comedy Central for two months.

At 14, he moved to Michigan with his family. He studied at Western Michigan University. After graduating, he began his career in radio at WRKR in Kalamazoo and made his first movie role, which was in The Rule of Attraction (2002) as Quinlivan in a scene that sadly ended up cut from the film.

Skyler Stone played Scarlett Johansson’s character’s husband in the flashback scenes for The Island (2005) and a cook in Waiting…. (2005). He joined the cast in Christopher Guest’s For Your Consideration (2006) as a trashy radio morning show host after Guest saw the “Cancun Coma” episode of Con (2005). Later, he joined the cast in the comedy film House Broken in 2009 as Danny DeVito’s son.

Skyler Stone sold a pilot called Skyler’s Revolution to Fox Broadcasting Company and Warner Bros in 2007. Also in 2007, wherein he made prank calls to celebrities, but they did not pick it up for a series. He appeared in the CSI: NY (2004) episode Youngblood. In 2010, he appeared in the sitcom Rules of Engagement (2007-13).

Stone is daily at the Laugh Factory, The Improv, and The Comedy Store, in Hollywood, California. He made his first national stand-up impression on The Late Late Show with Craig Ferguson on January 3, 2007. In 2013, he gave the voice for Scowler, a Pachyrhinosaurus in BBC Earth’s Walking With Dinosaurs: The Movie. Stone (2013). Currently, he lives in Sherman Oaks, California.
