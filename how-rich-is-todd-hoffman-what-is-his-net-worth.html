Todd Hoffman is an American reality television personality, YouTuber and gold prospector. He has an estimated net worth of US$7 million, which is attributed to his knowledge in gold mining. By season’s end, the Hoffmans had mined 1,644 ounces in Colorado, worth just under $2 million.

Who is Todd Hoffman?

Todd Hoffman is an American miner and reality television personality. He first gained recognition as the leader of a gold mining crew on “Gold Rush” which premiered in 2010. He was born on April 12, 1969 in Sandy, Oregon in the United States of America. His parents are Jack and Georgia Hoffman. Todd has one sibling named Tamra Hoffman.

Aside being on the screens for his knowledge in gold and as a miner, Todd is married to Shawna Hoffman and together the couple have a son, Hunter Hoffman.
