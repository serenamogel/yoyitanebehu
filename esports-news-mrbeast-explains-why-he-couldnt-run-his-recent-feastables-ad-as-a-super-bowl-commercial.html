7 million dollars was a lot of $, MrBeast explains why he couldnt run his recent Feastables

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, August 16, 2024

MrBeast took the internet by storm after making his cameo in the recent Super Bowl commercial. If this wasn’t surprising enough, the YouTuber made another revelation after releasing an advertisement for the latest addition to his Feastables.

In his recent Twitter post, Jimmy Donaldson unveiled the commercial for ‘Deez nuts’, the latest chocolate bar flavor added in Feastables. In addition to the video, he announced that this ad was originally meant to run as a Super Bowl commercial.

Explaining why the golden opportunity was missed, the popular content creator cited financial constraints. “7 million dollars was a lot of $,” claimed MrBeast while further adding that his audience should be satisfied with getting the advertisement here (Twitter).

The feastables commercial comes at a time when the memories of Super Bowl LVII are still afresh among NFL fans. With American National Football League concluding their biggest event of the season, MrBeast’s presence on the video led by Diana Flores, became a major attraction among the audience.
