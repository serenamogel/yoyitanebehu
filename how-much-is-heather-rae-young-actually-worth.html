How Much Is Heather Rae Young Actually Worth?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, May 5, 2024

Heather Rae Young became a household name for reality TV lovers by starring on Netflix's "Selling Sunset." The show is chock-full of multimillion-dollar homes, celebrity buyers and sellers, and one of the most flawless TV casts ever. Given that Young is one of the real estate agents who flaunts her designer clothes and luxury cars, it's only natural that her net worth is explored. The agent is worth an estimated $3 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. That number must be reflecting Young's earnings from selling houses as an agent and from her contract with Netflix.

Young's net worth may increase once she marries her fiance, Tarek El Moussa. The couple announced their engagement in July 2020 via Instagram. As he said on Instagram he "started with zero dollars, worked for everything," and the two certainly flaunt their lifestyle of private jets and high fashion. El Moussa is also a real estate agent, but he's mainly known for his HGTV renovation shows, "Flip or Flop" and "Flipping 101." Because of the success of his shows, El Moussa is now worth an estimated $10 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. So, unless there's a prenup in the works for the pair, Young may have some more millions in her bank account soon.
