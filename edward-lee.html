Biography

Edward Lee was born in Brooklyn, NY on July 2, 1972. Celebrity chef and winner of Food Network’s Iron Chef America. He was also a favorite on season nine of Top Chef: Texas and appeared on the third season of the PBS show The Mind of a Chef. He is also owner of restaurants 610 Magnolia, Milkweed and Succotash. He published his own cookbook called Smoke and Pickles, which received a positive review from celebrity chef Anthony Bourdain. He began cooking at the age of 11.
