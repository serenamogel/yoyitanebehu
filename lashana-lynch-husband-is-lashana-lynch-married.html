Lashana Lynch Husband: Is Lashana Lynch married?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, August 17, 2024

Lashana Lynch is a British actress known for her roles in film, television, and theatre. She was born on November 27, 1987, in Hammersmith, London, England. Lynch made her film debut in the 2012 drama film Fast Girls, a production presented at the 2012 Cannes Film Festival.

She later transitioned to film and television, with notable roles in the television series “Still Star-Crossed” and “Bulletproof,” as well as the films “Captain Marvel” and “No Time to Die,” where she plays the character of Nomi, the first Black female 00 agent in the James Bond franchise.

Lynch has been praised for her performances and her advocacy for greater diversity and representation in the entertainment industry. Among her accolades, she won the BAFTA Rising Star Award in 2022.
